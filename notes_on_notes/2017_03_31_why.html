Why take notes?

31st March, 2017

Given the huge volume of technologies available and their quick pace of development, I frequently jump back-and-forth between them, as one technology seems to be a better fit to my new project, or because of plain boredom. After a few months, I often forget how to do even the most basic tasks in the technologies I left behind.
I began writing a series of posts outlining the steps of getting a Django project up and running, and in the process noticed that taking notes itself and putting them online is not a simple and obvious process.
Since there are a myriad of options catering to every skill level for publishing content online, I will describe how I have been taking notes for my Django project.
I see the benefits of taking notes as:
  1. developing a quick reference to recreate, step-by-step, a useful product
  2. keeping track of a sequential thought process
  3. highlighting tricky conditions
  4. practicing and improving writing skills
  5. potentially helping out other readers
So, let's begin the process of writing notes!
