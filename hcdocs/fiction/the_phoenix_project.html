For immediate release

Steve Masters steps down from his role of chairman after 8 years.

Bob Strauss assumes the role of chairman.

The "Phoenix" program is a plan to tightly integrate its retailing and e-commerce channels.

Management movements that benefited the manufacturing plant (Ch. 7)

  • Theory of Constraints (improvements must be done in the bottleneck)
  • Lean production (Toyota Production System)
  • Total Quality Management

WIP (Work in process) is the silent killer. One of the most critical mechanisms in the management of any plant is job and materials release.

The Three Ways

  1. understand how to create fast flow of work as it moves from Development into IT Operations
  2. how to shorten and amplify feedback loops to fix quality at the source and avoid rework
  3. how to create a culture that simultaneously fosters experimentation, learning from failure, and understanding that repetition and practice are prerequisites to mastery

The four types of work (as stated by Erik, end of Ch. 7)

  • Business project work (Ch. 7)

  • Changes (Ch. 9)
