The Seeker of Nothing

The seven secrets

POWER

Life is but a journey upon
  which you embark.
As you descend from your
  eternal home,
you are each given a boon:

Choice.

This promise contains your power.

FREEDOM

Self-identity determines how
  the gift of choice is used,
thus the question, who are you?

In the answer, you will find
  your freedom.

Can you transcend outward appearances?

When you become truly aware of who you are, you will be free. 

PEACE

The universe is perfect,
  a flawless design.
If all that happens
  does not appear so,
there still remains
  a distance to be covered.

Walk ahead, and you will
  see perfection.

It is unavoidable.

This is the passage to peace.

REDEMPTION

Pain is an event which
  cannot be avoided.
Suffering is a process
  which can.

The light of awareness
  offers protection against
  the darkness of suffering.

This understanding
  guides you to redemption.

Suffering, unlike pain, is not an event, but a state of mind.

It can be avoided with perspective. It is the way you perceive things that causes happiness or sadness in one's life.

Use the gift of choice to change your perspective on the situation. 

HEALING

Surrender, and you will mend
  that which is broken.
Do not differentiate between
  the beaten heart and
  the shattered bone.

In this remedy,
  will healing be found.

The first step in the healing process is accepting that you are hurt. Allow the emotions to flow. 

DIVINITY

You have only one true enemy
  -- pride
You have only one place to go
  -- within

This realization, is Divinity

Find the activity that opens your heart and engage in it regularly. For example, preparing food. 

MAGIC

Be present in the only place
  of importance: the present.

Here is where your
  magic resides.
