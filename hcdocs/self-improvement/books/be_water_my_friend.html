Be Water, My Friend (by Shannon Lee)

The teachings of Bruce Lee

Empty your mind.
Be formless, shapeless, like water.
You put water into a cup; it becomes the cup.
You put water into a teapot; it becomes the teapot.
You put it into a bottle; it becomes the bottle.
Now water can flow, or it can crash!
Be water, my friend.

Introduction

Bruce Lee doesn't want you to be Bruce Lee. He wants you to be the best version of you that you can be.

Do not be tense, but ready; not thinking, but not dreaming; not being set, but flexible. It is being wholly and quietly alive, aware, and alert; ready for whatever may come.

The Water Way

Yip Man: Never assert yourself against nature. Never be in frontal opposition to any problem, but control it by swinging with it.

Bruce Lee's core tenet:

Using no way as way, having no limitation as limitation.

Being present is not enough. You must be aware, paying attention, and actively participating.

Of course, it's not possible to always be "on". Life is not always within our control. We get sick, tired, and tend to tune out. We are not meant to be perfect.
