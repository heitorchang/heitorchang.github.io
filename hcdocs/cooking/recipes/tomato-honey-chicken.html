Tomato Honey Chicken

Source: invented

Ingredients

  • 500 g cubed chicken (3-4 cm a side)
  • 2 cubed med. tomatoes
  • 1 med. carrot, sliced
  • 1 med. onion, cubed
  • 1 med. red bell pepper, 2 cm cubes

Procedure

  1. saute onion
  2. add chicken, saute all sides
  3. add all veggies
  4. add a little water, cover and simmer for 10 mins. stir once or twice
  5. add sauce: 60 mL soy sauce, 60 mL sake, 30 mL honey.
  6. boil for about 3 mins. to remove alcohol
  7. mix 1 tbsp cornstarch in cold water. add to pan and cook until liquid thickens.
