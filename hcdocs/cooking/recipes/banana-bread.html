Banana Bread

Source: Professional Baking, 7th ed.

Ingredients

Dry: mix with a fork

  • flour 250 g
  • sugar 100 g
  • bkg. powder 12 g
  • bkg. soda 2 g
  • salt 4 g

Liquid: in a separate bowl, mix

  • mash bananas first: 250 g
  • egg 100 g (2 eggs)
  • oil 90 g (or butter 82 g)

Chop

  • walnuts 60 g

Procedure

  1. Sift dry ingr. into liq. and add walnuts. Do not overmix.
  2. Bake at 200℃ for 25-30 mins.
