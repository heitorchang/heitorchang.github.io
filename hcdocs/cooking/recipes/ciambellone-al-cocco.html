Ciambellone al cocco

Source: Tutti a tavola YouTube (adapted)

Ingredients

  • 50 g coco ralado
  • 200 mL leite de coco
  • 150 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 40 mL sunflower oil
  • 325 g flour
  • 20 g baking powder

Procedure

  1. Mix coco ralado and leite de coco. Rest for 10 mins.
  2. Add sugar, mix.
  3. Add eggs one by one.
  4. Add oil.
  5. Mix flour and baking powder in a separate bowl.
  6. Sift over mixture.
  7. Grease donut-shaped pan and bake at 180 deg. C for 25-30 mins.
