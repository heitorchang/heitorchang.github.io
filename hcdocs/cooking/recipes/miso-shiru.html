Miso shiru

Source: Professional Cooking, 7th ed.

Ingredients

  • 500 mL water
  • 10 g Hondashi
  • 10 mL miso paste
  • handful of wakame seaweed
  • veggies of choice, such as carrots, daikon radish, or napa cabbage
  • soft tofu, cubed
  • green onion, sliced thin

Procedure

  1. Mix the Hondashi in the water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add veggies and cook them (carrots: 10-15 minutes, daikon: 5 minutes, napa cabbage: 3 minutes)
  3. Add the wakame and boil for 2 minutes (add it before the veggies finish cooking)
  4. Add the tofu and cook for 1 minute
  5. Remove from heat and separate a small amount of soup to mix the miso in. Return to the soup.
  6. Add the green onion.
