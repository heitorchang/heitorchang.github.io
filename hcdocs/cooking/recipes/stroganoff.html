Stroganoff

Source: Key

1 cebola, 1 cenoura, pimentao frango - cozinhar 10 mins com pouco de agua e tampado coloca creme de leite tempero: 3 colher de sopa de molho ingles, 1 de mostarda, 1 de ketchup, sal apenas esquentar

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, small cubes
  • chicken or beef strips

Sauce

  • 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • salt, about 1/4 tsp per 300 g of meat plus 1/4 tsp (for the veggies)

Procedure (for beef)

  1. saute onion
  2. add veggies, cook for around 5 minutes
  3. saute beef on all sides for a few minutes.
  4. add cream and sauce

Procedure (for chicken)

  1. saute onion
  2. add a little water and cook covered for 5 mins
  3. add veggies, cover and cook another 5 mins
  4. add cream and sauce
