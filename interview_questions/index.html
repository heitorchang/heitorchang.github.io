What is your biggest accomplishment? _____OPTIMIZATION OF HYDROELECTRIC ENERGY PAGE_______
The hydroelectric energy page's purpose is to display water flow forecasts across 44 geographic basins. I worked on the first version of the page alone, and over time the scope and number of forecast models tripled. Loading times grew significantly, to about 20 seconds.

It was time for an overhaul. By the time we saw the need to upgrade the page, the company grew from 6 to 14 people, so we had a dedicated front-end specialist and more meteorologists. I was coordinating with the two teams, and responsible for writing the APIs that connected the data produced by the scientists to the front-end client. I also helped with structuring the database. With our efforts we reduced the loading time from 20 seconds to around 3 seconds. I learned some Vue and Postgres techniques from my teammates.
