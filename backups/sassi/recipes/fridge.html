Gather these ingredients
  • 3 bay leaf
  • 1 medium bell pepper
  • 30 g butter
  • 20 cardamom
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 100 g cashew nut
  • 500 to 600 g (1 boneless breast) chicken breast
  • 20 ml coriander seed
  • 5 medium cloves garlic
  • 2 inches ginger
  • 1/2 lime
  • 5 tbsp masala
  • 100 ml optional mint
  • 1 large onion
  • 500 mL rice, white
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 750 mL water 
based on Grandpa Kitchen

marinate chicken with 1/2 lime's juice, 1 tsp salt, ground coriander seeds and spice powder (curry or masala)
boil cardamom in a little water, save water and discard cardamom
grate ginger and garlic to make paste
chop mint
chop tomato
dice carrots into 1 cm cubes
dice onion

1. brown onion
2. saute carrots
3. add tomato, bell peppers, 1 tsp salt, ginger garlic paste then add chicken marinade
4. add bay leaves, cardamom water and salt
5. wash and add rice. add water to cover everything
6. cover pan and lower heat
7. cook for at least 25 minutes (to cook rice)
8. add more water if needed
9. add chopped mint leaves near the end (optional)
10. add cashew nuts when serving (otherwise they get soft inside the pan)
